Web Development

Web development takes a lot of different forms, it's not always just company web sites. From custom web applications built ground up to complex public web sites that help you communicate your businesses' mission and offerings; JDU privides a breadth of services that can help your small to medium sized business.

We specialize in developing custom applications that allow you to manage workflow, data and internal/externals process while providing the infrastructure to support them. Through managed hosting of Application, Database and Communication Servers as well as comprehensive backup and reporting systems JDU provides a full sweep environment for your project to be deployed within.

Planning & Management

Planning & executing complex web and infrastructure projects can take and require a ide breadth of experience and knowledge in order to execute in a timely manner and with the best code quality.

Depending on the type and size of the required solution, JDU can provide its own small team of developers in order to execute or build a project-specific team from a pool of freelance and contract develoeprs in order to ensure that the developers on staff are proficient in the areas required to meet the expectations of your project.

Infrastructure

JDU utilizes cloud-based systems in order to provide rapid deployment and provisioning of resources for internal and external based proejcts. This allows us the ability to easily scale clients application resources in an on-demand environment in order to meet and exceed the growth requirements of your application.

As well as addressing scaling requirements, cloud based systems allow us to easily configure resource-specific systems (notification servers, bulkmail servers, dedicated database servers) on a variety of platforms at a much lower cost of ownership than traditional systems.

This also means that the high costs of implementing a custom software solution either for internal or external use are a thing of the past. JDU calculates the overall cost across the board of operating, maintaining and running your infrastructure stack and provides you with a flat monthly or yearly cost in order for your project to operate. As time goes by and your application becomes more popular or increases in usaeage your bill will reflect these changes, essentially providing you with a pay only for what you use billing experience.

Support

JDU provides a full software ticket management solution to all of its clients. This includes access to our knowledgebase of common issues and resolutions as well as the ability to submit tickets to our support people and have them tracked and periodically audited to ensure that we're providing the best service possible for your solutions.

Our support systems are provided to clients for short period after deployment of their solution into production (warranty period based on size and complexity of the application), after this period a long-term support contract is proffered at a eithe ra set rate or amount of hours per month based on the size and complexity of the solution.
